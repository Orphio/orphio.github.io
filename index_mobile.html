Frequently asked questions

Are there any forms other than an invoice?

Yes! Every Linen account comes with an invoice, work order, estimate, and expense report.

Can my team use Linen?

Absolutely. In fact, Linen works best when you have the whole team using it. Learn how to add your team

What happens after I fill out a form on Linen?

All of the forms you and your team fill out are securely stored in the cloud. Think of it as a private vault for your company. From there, you can review, download, and share the completed forms as you see fit.

How long are my forms stored in the cloud?

Forever. We believe that you have a right to your data, no matter how old it is.

Can I add my logo and company info to the forms?

Yep, you sure can. You can also add your social media links right on the form so you can better interact with your customers.

Are there plans for more forms in the future?

Yup. We're working on adding more forms to Linen as we speak. No official release date has been set yet.

What devices does Linen work on?

Linen works on Apple and Android phones/tablets.
